Internship Opportunities for 2nd Year Students

There are numerous opportunities for 2nd year students at IIT Delhi. I made this website to spread awareness regarding the opportunites availabe. It covers the programs available to students, primarily open for CS, MT, and EE branches (with some exceptions).

Research Intern

In our year, approximately 30 students secured foreign research internships in countries like France, Germany, Italy, and other European nations.

Pathways to Research Internships

  • Programs like EPFL, SURF, and others listed on this comprehensive GitHub repository
  • Directly mailing professors at research institutes like Inria, CISPA, etc.
  • Using resources like CSRankings or university department websites to find professors
  • Contacting professors who have previously mentored IIT Delhi students

Application Process

Start mailing professors early (beginning of September). Some professors give direct offers while others conduct interviews. Online internships are also available through the same process.

Pros

  • Enhances your CV
  • Excellent talking point in interviews
  • Opportunity to visit different countries
  • Valuable experience for personal development
  • Excellent preparation for higher studies or research

Cons

  • Less time to prepare for 3rd year internships
  • Success often depends on JEE Rank, CGPA, and luck

My Insight

Foreign research internships can be luck-based, often favoring those with high CGPAs and good JEE Advanced ranks. The process can be frustrating (some send 100+ emails while others succeed with 2-3), but persistence is key. Consider your aspirations before deciding to pursue this path.

Recommendation: Contact seniors who have completed research internships for specific guidance on when, where, and how to apply.

Jane Street's SEE IIT Program

SEE IIT Programme

Jane Street hosts a special edition of their SEE program for IIT students graduating in 2027/2028 at their Hong Kong office.

Deadline: 25/09/2024

When: 11-17 Dec 2024

Where: Jane Street's Hong Kong Office

Program Tracks

Quantitative Trading Track

Learn about mathematical and financial topics including probability, market structure, and arbitrage through lectures and mock trading games.

Software Engineering Track

Learn OCaml and functional programming from Jane Street software engineers, diving deep into computer science fundamentals.

Jane Street covers all travel expenses, housing, social events, and provides meal reimbursements.

My Insight

CV shortlisting followed by a coding test and interview. The process of selection happens in October. In our year, 12 students were selected from IIT Delhi.

Recommendation: Consult seniors for more specific information and preparation tips.

Optiver's Future Focus Program

FutureFocus Program in Mumbai

An all-expenses-paid exclusive opportunity for pre-penultimate university students with strong quantitative thinking skills.

Eligibility: Pre-penultimate students (graduating in 2027)

Where: Optiver Mumbai office

Program Dates:

  • Program One: 2–6 June
  • Program Two: 9–13 June
  • Program Three: 16–20 June

Note: Optiver covers travel and accommodation for students outside Mumbai.

Exceptional performers may secure offers for the 2026 Summer Internship program.

My Insight

CV shortlisting only. The process of selection happens in late April. In our year, approximately 18 students were selected from IIT Delhi, with 11 receiving internship offers after the program.

Recommendation: Consult seniors for more specific information.

HRT's Explore Program

Explore HRT Program

Hudson River Trading invites Year 2 undergraduate students in CS, mathematics, or related STEM fields to apply for this program at their Singapore office.

Dates: Thursday, May 15 – Friday, May 16

Location: HRT Singapore Office (travel and hotel provided)

What to Expect

  • Tech talks on software engineering (C++) and algorithm development
  • 1:1 sessions with technical team members
  • Informational presentations and office tour
  • Special dinner event

Eligibility Requirements

  • Undergraduates graduating between December 2026 and August 2027
  • Pursuing degree in Math, CS, Stats, Physics, or related field
  • Strong interest in technology and problem-solving
  • High proficiency in C++ or Python
  • Interest in full-time employment opportunities in Singapore

My Insight

Selection based on a test covering basic DSA and CP, followed by an interview with probability puzzles and a DSA question. The test was on 16th April and the interviews were from 20th to 25th April I think

In our year, only 1 student was selected from IIT Delhi, and 3 from all IITs combined.

IMC Trading's Launchpad Program

Launchpad Program

An exclusive two-day discovery programme for students graduating in 2027 interested in quantitative trading and engineering roles.

Application Deadline: April 7, 2025

Eligible Branches

Trading

B Tech/IDD/Dual Degree (All Branches)

Engineering

B Tech/IDD/Dual Degree (CSE & ECE)

Benefits

  • Apply problem-solving skills to real-world challenges
  • Hands-on experience with trading simulations
  • Technical workshops and projects
  • Dedicated mentor support
  • Networking opportunities
  • Standout performers fast-tracked to summer internship program

Note: Selected participants must be able to travel to Mumbai on May 20 for the event on May 21-22, 2025. IMC covers travel and accommodation.

My Insight

There was an online test held on 28th April I think. It was online so you can guess what happened.

  • NTSE Advanced level questions (sequence guessing, picture guessing)
  • Medium to Hard Probability questions
  • Very Hard Leetcode problem on strings

Test duration: 2 hours

Scholarship Opportunities

Quadeye Excellence Scholarship Test

Scholarship Specifications:

  • ₹3,00,000 for All India Winner*
  • ₹2,00,000 for Top 3 students
  • ₹50,000 for Top 30 students
  • ₹10,000 for Top 60 students

*One student eligible for only one award

Application Deadline: April 27th, 11:00 PM IST

Eligibility:

  • Full-time B.Tech/B.Tech+M.Tech Dual in CS, ECE, or M.Sc. in Maths and Computing
  • Final year students
  • Minimum CGPA: 8.5 by end of 6th semester (B.Tech) or 8th semester (Dual Degree)
  • No disciplinary actions or backlogs

My Insight

There was an online test help on 8th June I think. It was online so you can probably guess what happened.

Test Format

1.5 hour test with:

  • 40 NTSE-type logical reasoning questions
  • 2 easy coding questions
  • 20 questions on Computer Networks, Operating Systems, and C++

Many selected candidates received direct interviews during intern season.
